About

17 year old Brian Bradley aka The Astronomical Kid or Astro, was born on August 26, 1996 in Brownsville, New York. He grew up in a popular city of lyrical mcee’s like Jay-z and Biggie Smalls, just to name a few. Being surrounded by such great talent, Astro decided to follow in these artists footsteps in becoming the popular mcee he wished to be.

In 2011, Astro appeared as a contestant on the popular tv show X Factor USA. He made it to the top 10, where he was unfortunately voted off. He continued to chase his dreams and in 2013, he signed a deal with "X Factor" judge L.A. Reid over at Epic Records. The aspiring Brooklyn mcee has now reached many heights in his life already. He continues to put out great music and grow his fan base.
